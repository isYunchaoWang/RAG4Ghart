From 1970 to 1995, the chart illustrates the livestock production trends across seven different estates. Among them, Whispering Willows Estate showed a consistent and significant upward trend, becoming the highest producer by 1995. Sunstone Farms also displayed an increasing trajectory until the mid-1980s, after which it experienced a steady decline. On the other hand, Golden Harvest Cooperative, Misty Meadow Acres, Riverbend Plantation, and Ironwood Ranch consistently decreased throughout the period. Starfall Organics maintained a generally stable trend with minor fluctuations peaking in the mid-1980s before declining slightly. Notably, Whispering Willows Estate achieved the most significant growth in production, showcasing a robust and linear increase over the years.
