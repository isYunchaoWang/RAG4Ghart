The analysis of beverage sales data reveals different trends across three products: Bottled Water, Alcoholic Beverages, and Coffee. Bottled Water shows an initial increase, peaking early, followed by a decline and eventual stabilization. Alcoholic Beverages experienced a rapid rise, peaking mid-period before decreasing and flattening. Coffee sales remained relatively steady with slight growth. The highest growth was observed in Alcoholic Beverages at the peak, indicating a significant surge in demand during the mid-period. Overall, while Bottled Water and Alcoholic Beverages fluctuated, Coffee maintained a consistent sales trend, contributing to overall stability.
