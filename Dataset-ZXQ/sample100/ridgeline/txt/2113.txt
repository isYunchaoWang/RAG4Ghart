The consumer preference data reveals distinct patterns among three consumer groups: Budget Shoppers, Tech-savvy Consumers, and Ethical Consumers. Budget Shoppers show a rapid increase, peaking early, followed by a gradual decline and stabilization. Tech-savvy Consumers also peak early but experience a smoother decline. Ethical Consumers maintain a stable preference range with slight fluctuations throughout. No significant turning points are observed after initial peaks. Overall, Budget Shoppers demonstrate the most prominent initial growth, while Ethical Consumers exhibit the most consistent behavior.
