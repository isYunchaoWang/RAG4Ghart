The chart displays the Return on Investment (ROI) (%) across four different investment categories. Venture Capital shows a broad range of ROIs, peaking significantly around the 10-20% range indicating a higher variability and potential for substantial gains, albeit with risk. Commodities experience an initial dip followed by a recovery, reflecting a volatile investment path with potential gains primarily around the 10-15% range. Angel Investments display a moderate upward trend primarily clustering around the 5-15% ROI range, representing a steady and moderate return potential. Peer-to-Peer Lending remains generally stable with a predominance in the 0-10% range, suggesting lower risk and lower returns. Overall, Venture Capital and Commodities exhibit higher growth potential but also higher variance, while Angel Investments and Peer-to-Peer Lending offer more stable but moderate returns.
