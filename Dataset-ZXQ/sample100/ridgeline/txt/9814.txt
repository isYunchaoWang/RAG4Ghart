The analysis of the housing price fluctuations across different regions reveals several key insights. The Mountain Regions and Urban Redevelopment Areas exhibit the highest maximum values, indicating substantial price peaks, while the Metropolitan Areas maintain more stable and moderate prices. Luxury Residences demonstrate a notable increase, reaching a peak and subsequently stabilizing, suggesting a growth after initial fluctuations. Suburbs and Rural Areas display minor variations, indicating a stable pricing trend over time. Overall, the dataset reveals a blend of stability and fluctuations, with significant growth in certain regions, particularly Urban Redevelopment Areas and Luxury Residences. No significant turning points are observed in most areas, suggesting consistent trends in housing prices.
