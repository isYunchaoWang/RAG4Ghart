The analysis of consumer preferences reveals distinct patterns across different segments. Premium Brands experienced a peak in preference, which gradually diminished over time, suggesting an initial high interest that tapered off. In contrast, Convenience-Oriented preferences showed a relatively stable trend with minimal fluctuations, indicating consistent interest. Budget Shoppers exhibited a steady trend with minor undulations, reflecting a stable preference. Tech-savvy Consumers maintained a consistent level of interest with slight variability. Overall, while there are slight fluctuations, the preferences for Convenience-Oriented and Tech-savvy Consumers remain the most stable, whereas Premium Brands initially spiked and then declined.
