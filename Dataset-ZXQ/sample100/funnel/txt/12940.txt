The analysis of the funnel chart for sports event participation reveals a significant drop-off at each stage. The highest recorded participation is at the ticket purchase stage with 1,143 participants, which then decreases to 650 at event attendance. This downward trend continues through stages of fan engagement (362), post-event feedback (176), and culminates at merchandise purchase with 146 participants. Overall, the engagement process from ticket purchase to merchandise purchase shows a consistent decline, indicating opportunities to investigate drop-offs and enhance engagement strategies to retain participants throughout each stage.
