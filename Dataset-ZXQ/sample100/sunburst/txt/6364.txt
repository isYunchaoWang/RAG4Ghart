In 2023, the housing market is predominantly characterized by a strong presence of Residential Property, accounting for 42.3% of the market share. The primary subcategory within Residential Property is Single Family Homes at 29.11%, indicating a significant preference in this type. Commercial Real Estate holds the second-largest portion at 30.48%, heavily influenced by Retail Spaces (21.7%). The broad distribution shows notable diversity with balanced allocations across various residential and commercial categories, without any drastic fluctuations. The market displays stability with a gradual lean towards Residential Property, driven by Single Family Homes and complemented by distinct segments within each category, hinting at a stable and diversified market framework.
