In 2021, the employee distribution by category highlights significant emphasis on "Performance Management" and "Employee Development," each constituting 51.4% of the focus. These categories dominate the distribution, with other areas like "Workplace Wellbeing" (26.27%) and "Health & Safety" (20.5%) receiving moderate attention. Smaller percentages are allocated to categories within "Workplace Lifecycle" (22.33%), such as "Recruitment" (10.5%) and "Onboarding" (11.83%). The data shows a broad focus on both development and management, with a significant proportion also allocated to enhancing workplace conditions and safety. The overall trend suggests a balanced approach prioritizing key development areas while also addressing crucial wellbeing and safety aspects.
