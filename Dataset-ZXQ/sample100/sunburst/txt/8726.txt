In the analysis of the Sports Distribution by Category for 2024, the key findings indicate that Sports Media and Tech is the largest category at 40.39%, with a significant portion dedicated to Competitive Sports (31.45%) and Individual Sports (31.45%). The smallest categories are Twitch Coverage and Online Streaming, both at 6.14%. Overall, the trend suggests a stable distribution with no drastic fluctuations, indicating a balanced allocation across categories. Notably, Sports Technology at 28.61% shows moderate emphasis, impacting connected categories such as Analytics Tools at 13.4%. The preservation of these allocations underscores the industry's focus on both traditional media and emerging technologies in sports.
