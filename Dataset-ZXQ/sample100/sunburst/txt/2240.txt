The Education Distribution by Category for 2024 reveals that Informal Learning, at 42.68%, makes up the largest proportion of the distribution. Higher Education follows with 32.95%, primarily composed of Undergraduate programs. The collective share assigned to Informal and Higher Education suggests a significant emphasis on non-traditional and advanced studies. Community Learning, constituting 29.55%, indicates a strong focus on accessible education through various platforms. Formal Education represents a smaller segment, highlighting a potential shift towards more flexible learning environments. Digital Literacy and Online Education are notable in their growing relevance, indicating a push towards integrating technology into educational strategies. Overall, the trend suggests an increasing diversification in educational opportunities, with a significant lean towards informal and community-oriented learning pathways.
