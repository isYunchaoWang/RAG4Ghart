In 2021, the distribution of employee resources emphasizes "Workplace Wellbeing" as the leading category, accounting for 41.7% of the total. This indicates a strong organizational focus on employee health and morale. This is followed by significant investments in "Health & Safety" at 30.99% and "Onboarding" at 24.94%. Among all categories, "Candidate Evaluations" has the smallest allocation at 5.09%, highlighting potential areas for resource reallocation. This focus on wellbeing and safety suggests the company's commitment to fostering a supportive work environment, which is essential for sustaining workforce engagement and productivity.
