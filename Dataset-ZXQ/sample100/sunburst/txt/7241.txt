In 2024, the E-commerce distribution is prominently led by Fulfillment & Delivery and Payment & Logistics, both sharing the largest share at 37.47%. Customer Engagement is significantly emphasized, comprising 34.14%, followed by Customer Service categories like Live Support and Phone Help, each at 26.17% and 12.07%, respectively. Notably, video assistance holds a minimal share at 5.86%. The overall composition reflects a strategic emphasis on logistical efficiency and customer interaction, with a diverse allocation across various service aspects, ensuring robust support and engagement in e-commerce operations.
