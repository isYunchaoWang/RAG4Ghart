In 2024, agriculture production distribution reveals a balanced allocation across various categories. Crop Production leads with 37.92%, followed closely by Livestock at 32.26%, and Agricultural Inputs making up 29.82%. Within Crop Production, Conventional and Oilseeds hold the majority with 29.56% each, showcasing consistency in traditional farming practices. Poultry dominates Livestock with 21.66%, and Seeds are prominent at 29.82% among Agricultural Inputs. The data exhibits uniform distribution without significant fluctuations, indicating stability in agricultural priorities. Notably, the share of Free-Range and Intensive categories in Livestock are minimal, highlighting a lesser focus on these subcategories. Overall, the agricultural sectors maintain a steady distribution pattern with emphasis on conventional practices and poultry.
