In 2024, the education landscape is dominated by Informal Learning at 48.9%, with Online Education also holding a substantial share of 43.06%. Within Online Education, Skill Platforms contribute prominently, with LinkedIn Learning (14.18%) and Skillshare (13.11%) being the major components. The Higher Education sector, at 29.45%, is equally divided between Undergraduate and Research Universities. Meanwhile, Primary Education accounts for 21.66%, with Private Primary slightly leading over Public Primary. Medical Schools represent a smaller portion at 2.61%. Overall, the distribution reflects a strong emphasis on informal and online educational methods, indicating a shift towards flexible and accessible learning opportunities.
