In 2022, the tourism distribution was prominently oriented towards Hospitality Services (53.56%) and Destination Types (38.82%), with Dining Experiences (21.13%) and Nature Tourism (21.91%) also receiving significant attention. The smallest shares were in Cultural Dining Events (5.71%) and Traditional Restaurants (5.76%). The data suggests a balanced mix of interests, with no extreme fluctuations, indicating stable involvement across categories. Guided Tours and Travel Services, each at 25.82%, show considerable preference, likely driven by comprehensive experiences and convenience. The greatest growth potential might be in underrepresented areas such as Shopping Districts (6.29%) and Coastal Areas (8.02%), pointing to potential opportunities for future development and promotion.
