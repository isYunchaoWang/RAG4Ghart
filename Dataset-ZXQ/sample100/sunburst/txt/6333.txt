In 2023, the housing market distribution prominently features the rental market, which constitutes 40.44% of the overall distribution. Residential property and commercial real estate contribute 31.9% and 27.67%, respectively. The rental market is further divided, with long-term rentals accounting for 14.81% and short-term rentals 25.63%. Within short-term rentals, platforms like Booking.com and Airbnb lead with 10.26% combined, indicating a significant presence in the market. The vacation homes sector comprises 15.37%, showing a strong but balanced interest in various accommodation types. Significant categories within residential properties include single-family homes (23.5%) and multifamily homes (8.4%). The data reveal no major fluctuations, portraying a stable market with a consistent distribution across sectors, though rental demand is notably robust.
