In the 2021 employee distribution by category, Recruitment and Employee Lifecycle categories dominate with equal proportions of 55.65%, highlighting a significant focus on these areas within the organization. Employee Development, comprising 23.92%, and Workplace Wellbeing at 20.43% are also key areas of emphasis. Of note, Training Programs form 10.43% of the focus, with specific contributions from Online Learning (5.42%) and Skill Workshops (5.01%). Health & Safety is accounted for by three segments: Emergency Response (7.28%), Health & Safety initiatives (12.3%), and Ergonomics (5.02%). Work-Life Balance, Feedback, and Performance Management together form a substantial mix, emphasizing holistic employee-centric practices. Overall, the data suggests a balanced approach with substantial investments in recruitment and employee-centric development to enhance organizational health and productivity.
