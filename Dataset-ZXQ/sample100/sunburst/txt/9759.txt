In 2021, urban mobility captured the largest share of transportation distribution, accounting for 56.06%, with public transit (21.61%) and micromobility (34.45%) as major contributors. The key component within micromobility was e-scooters at 24.26%. Air transport, including commercial airlines, accounted for 22.64%, indicating a balanced presence alongside urban mobility. The data suggests a diverse yet stable distribution pattern in transportation categories, emphasizing the significant role of micromobility innovations and public transit systems in urban settings.
