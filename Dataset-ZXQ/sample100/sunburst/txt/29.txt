The 2024 agriculture production distribution analysis reveals that the majority focus lies in crop production at 42.24%, followed by agricultural inputs at 32.17%. Fertilizers constitute a significant portion of inputs, at 24.48%. Livestock and poultry have a balanced share of 25.6% each of the total production. The categorization within crop production highlights grains as the most dominant segment at 15.47%, with oilseeds contributing 26.77%, primarily from canola. Heirloom and hybrid seed varieties show similar distribution levels, each comprising approximately 7.69-7.1%. The production allocation reflects a diversified yet crop-centric strategy, with a notable emphasis on essential agricultural inputs.
