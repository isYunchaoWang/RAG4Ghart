In 2022, social media distribution was predominantly dominated by Platforms, constituting 47.67% of the total. Messaging Apps, specifically WhatsApp, followed with 36.29%. Video Platforms accounted for 31.26%, showcasing varied engagement with YouTube and TikTok being key contributors. Podcasting and its subcategories added to 12.63%, whereas Channels, Status, and Social Networks comprised relatively smaller shares. The data highlights a diverse yet concentrated engagement pattern across categories, with significant emphasis on platform-based and communication-focused applications.
