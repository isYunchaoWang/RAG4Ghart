In the 2023 housing market distribution, Residential Property and specifically Single Family Homes dominate with each accounting for 50.46% of the total market, highlighting a significant focus on residential over commercial real estate. The Rental Market represents 27.85%, with Short-Term Rentals comprising a notable portion at 17.85%. The Commercial Real Estate segment, including Retail Spaces and Offices, constitutes 21.69% of the market. The data reveals a noticeable skew towards residential properties, with a well-distributed focus on various rental markets. The market shows stability with Residential Property holding the largest share consistently, while Commercial Real Estate and Rental Markets share the remaining distribution without significant fluctuations or turning points.
