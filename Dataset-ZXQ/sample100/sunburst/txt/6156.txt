In 2023, the housing market distribution is significantly dominated by the rental market, accounting for 55.71% of the total. Within the rental segment, long-term rentals hold a substantial portion at 22.38%, while short-term rentals comprise 33.13%, with platforms like Airbnb and Booking.com playing significant roles, collectively representing around 32.53%. The commercial real estate sector accounts for 21.92% of the market, with office buildings being the most prominent at 14.44%. Residential properties, including single-family homes and apartments, collectively make up approximately 31.04% of the market. Overall, the data reveals a stable distribution trend, with rental and platform-based accommodations being pivotal in the housing landscape.
