In 2024, education distribution is heavily dominated by Informal Learning and Higher Education, accounting for 39.18% and 31.18%, respectively. Formal Education is also significant at 28.97%. Within these broader categories, Online Education and Undergraduate Programs play critical roles at 21.77% and 24.66%, respectively. Among online platforms, Skillshare leads at 10.79%, followed by Udemy at 5.72%. The data suggests a balanced approach to education with significant reliance on informal and online modes of learning, reflecting a diverse and evolving educational landscape.
