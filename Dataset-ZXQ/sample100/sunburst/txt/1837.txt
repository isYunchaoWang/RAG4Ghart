In the 2024 cultural engagement distribution, visual arts and creative expression are the dominant categories, both achieving the highest engagement percentage of 39.85%. The total participation is evenly distributed across several other categories, with cultural participation at 36.97% indicating significant involvement. Digital culture holds a notable position with 23.18%, highlighting the importance of online and virtual experiences, particularly in streaming culture (17.76%). Heritage engagement, while lower at 16.1%, still captures a substantial share. The lowest values are observed in site tours (9.21%) and museum visits (6.89%), with virtual museums and online exhibitions both at 5.42%. Overall, the distribution shows a diverse engagement landscape with a marked inclination towards visual and digital forms of participation.
