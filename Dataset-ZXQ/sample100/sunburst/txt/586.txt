In 2021, the primary focus areas in business distribution were Budgeting & Planning and Financial Management, both significantly leading with nearly 38% each. The other key areas of concentration included Technology Adoption and AI Integration, each around 19%. While Corporate Strategy had the smallest allocation, areas such as Data Strategy, Growth Models, and Innovation Strategy had moderate attention. The data indicates a strong emphasis on financial management and technological adoption, suggesting businesses are prioritizing financial stability and innovation in technology to drive growth.
