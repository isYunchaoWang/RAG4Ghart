In 2024, the energy distribution by category is led by Manufacturing (16.04%) and Mining (15.76%), indicating a significant portion of energy is consumed by industrial sectors. Solar energy (11.55%) and Thermal Plants (12.51%) are also substantial contributors, highlighting a shift towards alternative energy sources. Hydropower accounts for 10.24%, showcasing its role in renewable energy. The smallest shares are held by High Voltage Lines (5.12%) and Wind (5.08%). Overall, while traditional industrial consumption dominates, there is evident growth in renewable energy sectors, reflecting a gradual transition within the energy landscape.
