The transportation distribution for 2023 shows a diverse allocation across several categories, with Airport Infrastructure holding the largest share at 19.80%, followed closely by E-Scooters at 17.11%. The smallest share is observed in Public Transit at 5.31%. This suggests a trend towards diversification and modernization in transportation, accommodating growing demand for efficient and sustainable options.
