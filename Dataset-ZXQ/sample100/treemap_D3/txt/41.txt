In 2020, the food industry was primarily distributed across various categories, with Regional Hubs representing the largest share at 19.33%. This was followed by Livestock (12.93%) and Ice Cream (11.25%). The smallest contributions came from Slaughtering (5.99%) and Crop Farming (5.22%). The overall distribution is varied, with no single category dominating the landscape excessively, indicating a diverse industry structure. This diversity suggests a stable spread across multiple categories with Regional Hubs slightly leading, indicative of a developing focus on broadening logistical and infrastructural capabilities in the industry.
