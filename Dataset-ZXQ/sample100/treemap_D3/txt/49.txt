In 2022, the distribution of healthcare resources was led by Health Informatics (14.57%), followed closely by Primary Care (13.73%) and Health Surveillance (13.54%). These categories significantly outweighed others such as Medical Devices, which received the least allocation at 5.43%. The data reveals a diverse distribution strategy, focusing on both preventive and primary care, with moderate investment in areas like Patient Awareness and Insurance Models. The trend indicates a balanced approach to enhance different facets of the healthcare system, highlighting a strategic emphasis on essential services while also supporting regulatory and professional training initiatives.
