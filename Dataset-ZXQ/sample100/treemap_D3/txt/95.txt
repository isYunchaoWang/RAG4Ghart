In 2023, the "Tourism Distribution by Category" is led by Fine Dining and Rail & Road travel, which are almost neck-and-neck at 14.55% and 14.19%, respectively. Adventure Tours (10.72%) and Local Cuisine (8.67%) also capture significant shares. Meanwhile, categories like Budget Hotels (5.01%) and Mountain Destinations (5.15%) have the smallest shares. This distribution suggests a diverse tourism interest, with a strong inclination towards dining and transportation, while cultural and nature-based tourism occupy moderate portions. This balance reflects a multifaceted approach to tourism preferences in 2023.
