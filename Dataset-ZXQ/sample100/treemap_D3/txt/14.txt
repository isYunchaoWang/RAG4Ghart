In 2024, Virtual Museums accounted for the largest share of cultural activities at 25.65%, indicating a significant preference for digital engagement. Dance followed with 15.56%, while Theatre represented 12.08% of the distribution. Site Tours and Museum Visits held notable shares at 9.24% and 7.83%, respectively. Streaming Culture and Visual Arts contributed moderately with 7.62% and 6.17%. In contrast, NFT Artworks, Local Festivals, and International Events had smaller shares, ranging from 5.10% to 5.61%. The overall distribution reflects a diverse interest in both digital and traditional cultural experiences, with digital avenues showing prominent engagement.
