In 2022, the housing market distribution was primarily dominated by "Houses for Rent" (21.65%) and "Shopping Centers" (21.16%), indicating a balanced demand between residential leasing and commercial spaces. "Apartments" and "Short-Term Rentals" each accounted for a modest share of the market, comprising 6.47% and 6.58%. The presence of "Street-Level Shops" (7.62%) and "Office Buildings" (5.02%) suggests a stable but smaller investment in commercial real estate. This distribution highlights a multifaceted market with varied investment opportunities across residential and commercial categories.
