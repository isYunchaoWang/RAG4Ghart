In 2024, the distribution of cultural activities by category highlights several key insights. Virtual Museums lead with the highest participation at 25.65%, indicating a strong interest in digital experiences. Dance (15.56%) and Theatre (12.08%) also show significant engagement, reflecting continued popularity in these traditional performing arts. Site Tours (9.24%) and Museum Visits (7.83%) add to the diversity of cultural interests, suggesting a balanced appeal between physical and digital realms. The smallest shares are observed in International Events (5.10%) and NFT Artworks (5.15%), pointing to niche interests. Overall, the data suggests a diverse distribution with a notable inclination towards virtual and performing arts, underscoring evolving consumer preferences in cultural participation.
