In 2024, the energy distribution landscape is dominated by manufacturing (16.04%) and mining (15.76%), followed by thermal plants (12.51%) and solar energy (11.55%). Key renewables like solar, hydropower (10.24%), and wind (5.08%) compose a notable portion of the mix, highlighting a diversified energy portfolio. Residential consumption is relatively low at 6.63%. The smallest shares are seen in smart grids (5.94%), high voltage lines (5.12%), oil (5.46%), and coal (5.68%). Overall, the data reflects a balanced approach, with considerable representation from both traditional and renewable energy sources, indicating a transitional phase towards sustainable options.
