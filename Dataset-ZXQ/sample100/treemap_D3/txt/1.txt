In 2020, heirloom seeds constituted the largest segment of agriculture production distribution at 23.76%. Broilers followed at 14.32%, and other significant categories included egg layers at 9.62% and cattle at 9.87%. Meanwhile, hybrid seeds and canola had similar shares of 9.38% and 9.27% respectively, while fertilizers accounted for 5.28%. The smallest shares were observed in soybean, rice, and wheat, averaging around 6.10%. The data shows a stable distribution across categories, with no extreme fluctuations or turning points. This indicates a balanced approach in agricultural production distribution.
