In the 2022 Housing Market Distribution, the key categories are Houses for Rent (21.65%) and Shopping Centers (21.16%), both nearly comprising the same share of the market. Duplexes (12.60%) and Single Family Homes (8.70%) also represent significant portions. Smaller segments include Short-Term Rentals (6.58%), Apartments (6.47%), Street-Level Shops (7.62%), and Office Buildings (5.02%). The distribution does not indicate a clear overall trend but highlights a diverse allocation across commercial and residential categories, with Houses for Rent and Shopping Centers jointly dominating the landscape.
