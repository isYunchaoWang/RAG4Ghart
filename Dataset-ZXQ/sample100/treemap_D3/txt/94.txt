In 2023, the largest categories in tourism distribution are Fine Dining (14.55%) and Rail & Road (14.19%), indicating strong interest in culinary experiences and land-based travel. The smallest categories are Budget Hotels (5.01%) and Mountain Destinations (5.15%), suggesting relatively lower engagement in cost-effective accommodations and mountainous areas. The overall distribution shows a balanced interest across diverse categories; however, no single category shows a significant spike or decline, indicating a stable trend with varied interests catering to different tourist segments. This reflects a multifaceted tourism industry where preferences are evenly dispersed across various experiences.
