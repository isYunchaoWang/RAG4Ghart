In 2023, wind energy holds the largest share of renewable energy sources at 21%, followed by hydropower at 20%, and wave energy at 17%. Geothermal and biomass both represent significant portions at 16% and 15%, respectively, while solar energy comprises 11%. The distribution reflects diverse utilization of renewable resources, with wind and hydropower as leading contributors. This balanced allocation suggests a robust approach to leveraging various renewable sources to meet energy needs.
