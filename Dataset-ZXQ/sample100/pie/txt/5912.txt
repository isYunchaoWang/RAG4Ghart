The income distribution by job level in 2023 shows that the largest portion of income is held by Executives, accounting for 25% of the total. Internships follow with 23%, and Freelancers with 19% of the share. Both Contractors and Mid-Level workers account for 11% each. This data highlights a significant concentration of income at the executive level, with relatively balanced distribution among other categories, except for a noticeable gap between Executives and Internships compared to the rest.
