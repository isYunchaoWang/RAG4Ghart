In 2024, consumer purchasing behavior is primarily characterized by planned purchases, which account for the largest share at 21%. This is followed by discount seeking at 18% and brand loyalty at 17%. Impulse buying is the least significant category at 9%. Both price sensitivity and in-store purchases are equally represented at 12%, while online shopping is slightly lower at 11%. The distribution suggests a diverse range of purchasing motivations with a notable emphasis on planned and discounted buying. This trend indicates a consumer preference towards strategic and value-driven purchasing decisions.
