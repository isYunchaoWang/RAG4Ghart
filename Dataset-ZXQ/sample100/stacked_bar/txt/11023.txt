The analysis of video platform viewership reveals that Facebook and Amazon Prime dominate with the highest total views, peaking significantly above other platforms. Conversely, platforms like Vevo and TikTok report lower overall views, indicating variance in user engagement. The trends suggest a mixed pattern, where some platforms show balanced growth in both entertainment and news segments, while others display skewed growth. The most significant growth is observed in platforms with a large increase in news viewership, pointing to a shift in viewer preference. These insights indicate strategic opportunities for platforms to capitalize on trending content demand.
