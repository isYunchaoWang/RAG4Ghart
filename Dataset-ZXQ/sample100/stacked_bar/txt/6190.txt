The data on hospital visits across five towns from 2010 to 2020 reveals an overall trend of increased fluctuations over the decade. The maximum number of visits is observed in 2011, and the minimum in 2017. The trend includes significant growth at periods such as between 2010 and 2011 and after 2017. However, there is a notable dip in visits during 2017. Overall, while there are fluctuations, a general recovery and stabilization in visits are seen by 2020.
