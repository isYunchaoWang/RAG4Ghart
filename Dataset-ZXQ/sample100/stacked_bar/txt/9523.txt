The analysis of the monthly online sales revenue reveals that there is significant variability in performance between the Electronics and Sports categories. Notably, the month with the highest sales, driven primarily by the Sports category, occurred in June. Throughout the year, a pronounced peak in the Sports category is evident, particularly in June and August, while the Electronics category shows more modest fluctuations. The overall trend does not follow a linear pattern and is instead characterized by significant peaks and troughs, specifically for the Sports category. No clear upward or downward trend is identified, indicating a stable yet fluctuating pattern of sales across the months.
