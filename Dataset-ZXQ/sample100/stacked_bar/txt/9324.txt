In 2023, the monthly online sales revenue data across different product categories showed significant fluctuations. The highest monthly sales occurred in June, while February recorded the lowest. Overall, the trend reveals a generally increasing sales pattern from January to June, peaking in June, followed by a decrease and another rise towards the year's end. Notably, sales surged during May and June, driven primarily by increased garden and grocery sales, indicating seasonal influence as a critical growth factor. The year concluded with a recovery in December, indicating a potential rise in consumer spending during the holiday season. This analysis suggests a need for strategic planning to leverage peak sales periods effectively.
