The analysis of housing prices for various property types from 2006 to 2023 reveals significant trends and fluctuations. The maximum recorded prices were in 2011, while the minimum was observed in 2019. The overall trend has shown fluctuations, with a notable peak in 2011 followed by a decline, reaching a low in 2019, and a subsequent recovery by 2023. The growth pattern is not linear, showing a sharp rise until 2011, then a drop, and partial recovery indicating cyclical movements rather than sustained growth. The highest growth occurred between 2006 and 2011. Notably, the market appears to have turned around post-2019, with prices increasing again by 2023. Overall, the housing market experienced both volatility and recovery within the analyzed period.
