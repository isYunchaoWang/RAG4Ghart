The analysis of patient care metrics across various hospitals reveals significant variability. Key metrics such as readmission rates, staff-to-patient ratios, medication error rates, preventive care rates, average length of stay, and patient satisfaction demonstrate both high and low values, indicating diverse performance levels. Variations in patient satisfaction highlight areas needing attention.
