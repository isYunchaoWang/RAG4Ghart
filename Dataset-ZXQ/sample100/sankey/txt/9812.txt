The data presents an analysis of purchases within an omni-channel retail ecosystem segmented by customer profiles and sales channels. The key insights indicate that Adults channel the highest number of purchases through the Online Store with a total of 6,283 transactions, marking a significant contribution compared to other segments. Professionals prefer the Third-Party Marketplace, noting 5,722 purchases, while Parents show a balanced distribution with almost equal numbers through both channels. The ecosystem demonstrates a balanced, stable purchase behavior, with Adults showing the highest preference for the Online Store. No abrupt turning points or spikes were observed, suggesting a steady engagement across all segments and channels.
