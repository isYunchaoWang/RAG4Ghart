The Omni-channel Retail Purchase Ecosystem shows a diverse distribution of purchases across various product categories, regions, customer segments, channels, and marketing campaigns. Groceries and clothing are the top-selling categories, with groceries slightly leading. The Asian market contributes significantly, particularly among teens and young adults. Mobile apps and third-party marketplaces are the leading sales channels, with flash sales being the most effective marketing campaign. The data indicates stable transaction patterns with a minor peak in grocery and mobile app statistics, suggesting a current consumer preference towards digital shopping channels and time-sensitive promotions.
