The data represents the distribution of sports event engagements across various sports types, event types, engagement channels, and audience segments. Basketball leads in popularity with a maximum value of 10,177 events, representing the highest engagement, while Baseball and Cricket follow with significantly lower numbers. The overall distribution suggests a stable engagement trend across these sports, with notable peaks for events like the Basketball Finals and Tournament.

The most significant audience engagement channel is Social Media, largely catered to Local Fans, while Fan Meetups engage International Fans considerably. Mobile Apps and Fantasy Leagues facilitate varied engagement but to a lesser extent. A notable point is the high engagement in Fan Meetups and Mobile Apps for the Basketball Finals, indicating a strategic area of growth. The sports and event landscape is driven by maximizing engagements through diversifying channels tailored to specific audience segments.
