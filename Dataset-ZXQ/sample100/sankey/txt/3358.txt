The chart illustrates the flow of students from various support services toward post-education paths. Key metrics show that Career Services directed the majority (2,455) toward Graduate Studies, while Academic Advising contributed to both Entrepreneurship (371) and Graduate Studies (522). Counseling had lower numbers, with 117 moving towards Entrepreneurship and 843 towards Graduate Studies. The overall trend indicates a significant preference for Graduate Studies, showing a stable pattern across services, with some fluctuation evident in the routes from Academic Advising. No significant turning points were observed, as the flow remained directed towards established post-education paths.
