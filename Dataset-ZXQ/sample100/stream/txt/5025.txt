From 1950 to 1975, the cost per hire exhibited an overall increasing trend. Initially, there was a modest rise, followed by more pronounced growth, particularly influenced by Lumina Solutions. While Zenith Enterprises and AstraCorp showed relatively stable contributions with slight fluctuations, Lumina Solutions experienced the most significant growth, marking a clear upward trajectory in the total cost. The most notable expansion occurred in the late 1960s to early 1970s, indicating a substantial increase in hiring costs during this period.
