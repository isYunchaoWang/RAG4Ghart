The online sales volume from 1983 to 1993 shows a general increasing trend with noticeable fluctuations. Initially, sales increase in the period between 1983 and 1988, marked by significant growth in some categories such as Rural Buyers and Urban Shoppers. However, there is a notable decline around the late 1980s followed by a recovery and stabilization through to 1993. Key turning points include a peak in 1988 and a low around 1990 before another rise towards the end of the period. The trend overall aligns with a pattern of initial growth, a mid-decade dip, and then recovery, emphasizing shifts in consumer demographics and spending patterns over time.
