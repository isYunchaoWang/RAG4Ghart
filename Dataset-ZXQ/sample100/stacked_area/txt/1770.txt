The overall trend of art gallery visitors from 1989 to 2010 reveals moderate growth with fluctuations. Visitor numbers show a pattern of increase interspersed with some declines, leading to a shape characterized by growth after fluctuations. The maximum visitor volume is just above 1.2 million, while the minimum is slightly below 0.8 million. Visitor numbers have followed an upward trend over the period, indicating a positive trajectory. Notably, there are peaks every few years, suggesting seasonal or event-related spikes in attendance. The highest growth is observed towards the end of the period, highlighting a strong finish. Overall, the steady increase in visitors indicates growing interest and engagement with the art galleries over the examined years.
