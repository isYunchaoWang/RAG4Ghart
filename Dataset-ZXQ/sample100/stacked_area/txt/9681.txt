From 1976 to 1987, driver satisfaction steadily increased across all categories observed: International Routes, Freight Train, Long-Haul Operators, and Last-Mile Couriers. The data exhibit linear growth, without significant fluctuations or turning points, indicating a consistent improvement in driver satisfaction. The most substantial growth is noticeable in Last-Mile Couriers and Long-Haul Operators, contributing significantly to the cumulative satisfaction index. Overall, the positive trend reflects a continuous enhancement in driver satisfaction over the years.
