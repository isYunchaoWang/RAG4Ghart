In 2024, the distribution of cultural activities by category reveals that Artwork Sales is the dominant segment, constituting 86.42% of the total. Within this, the Primary Market accounts for 73.42%, with Galleries, particularly Contemporary art, being significant contributors. Art Fairs follow at 19.07%, indicating strong regional interest. The Secondary Market occupies a smaller share at 13.00%, with Auction Houses and Art Funds closely matched in their contributions. Public Art and Art Exhibitions represent a combined 13.58%, suggesting a modest focus on community and participatory events. The overall distribution indicates a robust market for artwork sales, with a more focused emphasis on contemporary and regional segments.
