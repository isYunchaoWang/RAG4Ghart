In 2021, Agriculture Production Distribution was dominated by Crop Systems, comprising 100% of the data. Within this category, Precision Planting held the largest share at 80.29%, with the greatest concentration in Seeders, Row Units, and Depth Control (each 56.81%). Parallel Linkages represented 47.73%, showcasing extensive interest. Fertigation Systems accounted for 23.48%, with Dosing Mechanisms and Venturi Injectors both equally contributing. Anti-Clog Nozzles also had a notable presence at 14.54%. Irrigation Tech occupied 19.71%, with Drip Networks and Emitter Design both at 13.18%. The data reflects a significant emphasis on precision technologies, indicating a trend towards refined, efficient agricultural processes with a focus on maximizing planting accuracy and irrigation effectiveness.
