In 2024, the distribution of cultural activities is heavily dominated by the category of Public Art, which takes up 78.88% of the total cultural activities, with Urban Sculpture being the most significant subcategory at 69.93%. Art Exhibitions, which include Online and Gallery Shows, account for 10.80%, showcasing a balanced mix of traditional and digital mediums. Artwork Sales, comprising the Primary and Secondary Markets, constitute the remaining 10.32%. The data indicates a strong emphasis on public and community arts, with a notable, albeit smaller, presence of commercial art sales. Overall, the trend highlights the growing importance of accessible public art in the cultural landscape.
