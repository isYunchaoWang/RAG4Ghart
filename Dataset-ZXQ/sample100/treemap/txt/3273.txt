The Education Distribution by Category for 2021 indicates that the most significant portion is occupied by Experiential Learning Systems, which makes up 75.85% of the total. Within this category, Fieldwork Tech, Mobile Sensing, and IoT Kits each account for 49.73%, with Edge Computing following at 42.41%. Lab Simulations, comprising 26.12%, is significant within Experiential as well, particularly VR Environments and Haptic Feedback at 16.76% each. Pedagogy accounts for 24.15%, led by Competency-Based and Micro-Credentials, each at 18.88%. The analysis reveals a clear preference for experiential learning methods, with a broad distribution across various innovative tech-driven categories, reflecting a strong integration of emerging technologies into educational systems.
