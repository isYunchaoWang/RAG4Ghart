In the 2021 food industry distribution by category, the Processing Lines and Thermal Systems dominate with both having a substantial share of 74.26%. Blast Chillers and its subsections, including Refrigeration Circuits and Compressor Types, closely follow with 55.67%, with Screw and Scroll Compressors making up significant portions of this category at 30.72% and 24.95% respectively. Packaging Systems account for 25.74%, with Filling Machines being the largest sub-category at 16.96%. The data exhibits a stable distribution without significant fluctuations or turning points, suggesting a mature industry structure. Overall, the food industry equipment market is heavily skewed towards processing and thermal systems, reflecting their critical role in industry operations.
