In 2021, the distribution of education by category predominantly favored experiential methods, which comprised 89.78% of the total. This included significant contributions from Fieldwork Technology and Lab Simulations, with Fieldwork Tech being the largest single component at 47.45%. Within Fieldwork Tech, Mobile Sensing and IoT Kits were equally significant. Lab Simulations also had a notable presence, driven primarily by VR Environments and Haptic Feedback. Pedagogy accounted for a smaller share at 10.22%, with Active Learning contributing 5.18%. The data indicates a strong preference for technology-driven experiential learning approaches within educational systems.
