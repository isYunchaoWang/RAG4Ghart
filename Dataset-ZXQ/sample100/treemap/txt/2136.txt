In the Cultural Activities Distribution for 2024, Artwork Sales dominate with an 80.66% share, highlighting its significance in the sector. Within this, the Secondary Market is substantial at 55.46%, with Art Funds, particularly Investment Grade pieces, marking the peak at 31.79%. The Primary Market contributes 25.20%, mostly through Galleries, with traditional art slightly eclipsing contemporary forms. Public Art at 13.42% and other activities like Art Exhibitions (5.92%) and Community Projects (6.37%) show the sector's diversity. The distribution demonstrates a balanced yet prominent focus on traditional and investment-grade categories.
