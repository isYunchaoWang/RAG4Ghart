The chart on Education Distribution by Category for 2021 reveals that Learning Systems is distributed between Experiential (58.04%) and Pedagogy (41.96%), with Experiential leading in emphasis. Within Experiential, Lab Simulations (48.83%) and Mobile Sensing (9.21%) show significant focus, highlighting the integration of technology in education. Pedagogy focuses primarily on Competency-Based and Micro-Credentials, both at 28.78%. Active Learning, though smaller at 13.18%, showcases an emphasis on Project-Based and Collaborative Tools at 7.34% each. Overall, the distribution reflects a steady, balanced emphasis on experiential and pedagogical approaches, catering to evolving educational trends with a marked inclination towards technology and practical skills development.
