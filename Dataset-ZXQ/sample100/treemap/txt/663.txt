In 2021, Crop Systems were divided into several categories, with Irrigation Tech representing a significant portion at 74.70%. Within this, Pivot Systems accounted for 64.34%, including major subcategories like Control Modules, Water Flow, and Valve Types, all at 49.14%, with Ball Valves contributing 41.18% and Solenoid Valves 7.96%. Precision Planting was 25.30%, with Fertigation Systems, Dosing Mechanisms, and Venturi Injectors each contributing 15.40%. The overall production distribution shows a concentration in specific areas, indicating a strategic focus on irrigation and precision planting technologies. The distribution reflects a stable trend prioritizing key components in agricultural efficiency and precision.
