Between 1990 and 2006, student enrollment across five institutions showed varied trends. Aethelgard School of Arts consistently experienced strong linear growth, achieving the highest enrollment levels among the institutions by 2006, with a noticeable upward trajectory throughout the period. Celestial College showed moderate growth, eventually surpassing Borealis Graduate School in enrollment around the year 2000. Borealis Graduate School saw a gradual decline after initially steady numbers, indicating a turning point in its enrollment trend around the year 2000. Silverhaven Polytechnic had initial declines but rebounded with improvement towards the end of the period. Meridian Academy displayed minor fluctuations, maintaining a stable trend throughout. The analysis highlights that Aethelgard School of Arts is the most rapidly growing institution, whereas Borealis Graduate School's decline marks a significant shift in enrollment patterns during the observed years.
