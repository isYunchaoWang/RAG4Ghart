The transportation and logistics data heatmap demonstrates an overall positive trend across various metrics such as Capacity, Compliance, Cost, Efficiency, Labor, Reliability, Speed, Sustainability, and Technology from Air to Warehousing services. Maximum values generally converge around the Intermodal and Last Mile sectors, showing strong performance, while the minimum values appear to be associated with the Air and Warehousing sectors. The average values show steady growth, indicating improvement across most sectors. Notably, substantial growth is noticeable in Technology and Intermodal, suggesting significant advancements, while no pronounced turning points are evident, signifying a linear growth trend.
