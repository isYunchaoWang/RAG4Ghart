From 1967 to 1995, the overall trend of streaming views exhibited significant growth, reaching its peak towards the end of the period. The data shows a consistent increase, particularly noticeable after 1983, where growth accelerated in a more exponential manner. The maximum total views occurred in 1995, while the initial years recorded the lowest numbers. Significant growth was driven primarily by Luminara Play and EchoWave Entertainment, with marked increases in viewership contributing to the upward trend. Notably, a turning point appeared around the early 1980s, catalyzing the following rapid increase. StarburstTV and CrimsonCast also contributed to the upward momentum, maintaining steady growth throughout.
