Between 2005 and 2018, the average watch time showed a significant upward trend, increasing from about 80 minutes to approximately 175 minutes. The growth was not linear; after a period of fluctuations in the early years, there was a steady increase, especially notable from around 2013 onwards. The expansion was predominantly driven by mobile users, with desktop and new users contributing to a lesser extent. The most substantial growth occurred post-2013, indicating a turning point, likely reflecting the broader adoption of mobile technology. The chart highlights a shift in user behavior favoring mobile platforms for content consumption.
