From 1996 to 2021, the alumni giving rate shows a significant upward trend, characterized by a steady increase in contributions across all institutions. The aggregate giving rate begins near 50% in 1996 and climbs to over 150% by 2021. This growth is largely linear with notable accelerations post-2010, especially between 2016 and 2021, suggesting consistent enhancement in alumni engagement. The most substantial growth is observed for the Arcane University Alumni Association, contributing considerably to the overall increase. The data indicates a robust upward momentum in alumni contributions, signifying effective engagement strategies over the years.
