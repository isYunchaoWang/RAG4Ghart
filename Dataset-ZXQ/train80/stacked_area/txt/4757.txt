The cost per hire from 2007 to 2024 shows a consistent upward trend, with notable increases after 2015 and a sharper rise from 2020 onwards. The maximum cost is observed at the end of the period, while the minimum is at the beginning. There is a clear pattern of growth with fluctuations in certain years, suggesting regular increases in hiring costs across the analyzed companies. This growth appears to accelerate in more recent years, highlighting significant rising expenses in recruitment processes.
