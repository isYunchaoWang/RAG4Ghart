The employee recruitment process funnel indicates a gradual decrease at each stage. The process starts with 100% of applications submitted, and there is a significant drop at each subsequent stage: Phone Screen (65%), HR Interview (46%), Technical Interview (37%), and finally, Offer Extended (31%). The highest transition loss occurs from the Phone Screen to the HR Interview stage, showing a 19% drop. The overall trend depicts a decreasing funnel shape, suggesting that a considerable attrition occurs through each stage. This analysis points to potential areas for improvement in retention and conversion rates at various stages of the recruitment process.
