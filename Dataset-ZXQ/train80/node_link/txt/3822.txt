The graph of culinary supplier relations consists of five nodes, representing key distributors, and eight edges detailing different types of relations: Brand Franchise, Ingredient Supply, Logistics Distribution, Menu Cross Listing, and Retail Partner. All nodes are part of a single connected component, indicating a cohesive network with no isolated parties. The top three most influential nodes based on degree centrality, which signifies their number of connections, are Olive & Thyme, Crimson Table, and The Golden Fork. This insight highlights their pivotal roles in maintaining and leveraging supplier relationships across the network. Overall, the network visualizes a well-integrated system of culinary distributive collaborations, spearheaded by these central nodes.
