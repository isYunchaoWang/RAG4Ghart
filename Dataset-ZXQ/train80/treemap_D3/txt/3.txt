In 2020, the largest category in agricultural production was Heirloom Seeds, contributing 23.76% to the total. Broilers followed with 14.32%, and Egg Layers accounted for 9.62%. The production was less dominant in areas such as Fertilizers at 5.28%. The distribution showed a variety of production areas with significant contributions from each category, indicating a diverse agricultural sector. This diversification helps in reducing dependency on any single category, promoting stability in the agricultural market. There were no drastic shifts or turning points observed, indicating a stable landscape in agricultural production for the year.
