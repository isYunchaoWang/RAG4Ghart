In 2022, email campaigns represented the largest share of e-commerce distribution categories with 21.49%, followed by social media ads at 10.20%. The smallest share was seen in e-wallets, accounting for 5.74%. The distribution is relatively balanced across various categories, with no single area dominating by a wide margin. Notable insights include a significant investment in digital advertising and logistics, with substantial contributions from last-mile delivery and warehousing. This distribution highlights the diverse strategies employed within the e-commerce sector to enhance market reach and operational efficiency.
