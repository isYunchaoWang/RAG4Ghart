In the 2022 e-commerce distribution analysis, Email Campaigns emerged as the largest category at 21.49%, signifying its prominence in digital marketing strategies. Conversely, E-Wallets were the least represented at 5.74%, indicating opportunities for growth in the payment sector. The data presented a diverse spread with no single category overwhelmingly dominant, hinting at a balanced investment across various e-commerce facets. Notably, while there were no sharp turns or drastic shifts, categories such as Social Media Ads (10.20%) and Customer Service (6.73%) showcased gradual integration into overall strategies. The spread reflects a multi-faceted approach to enhancing the e-commerce ecosystem, balancing marketing, logistics, and customer engagement.
