In 2024, social media distribution is diversified across various platforms and categories, with notable allocations. Spotify leads with the highest distribution at 17.01%, followed by Memes at 13.18%, and WhatsApp at 11.26%. Categories like Likes, TikTok, and Apple Podcasts have the lowest distributions, each below 6%. The overall distribution shows a balanced allocation across different categories with no single category overwhelmingly dominating, indicating a varied use of platforms. This suggests a trend towards diversified media consumption habits among users.
