In the 2022 e-commerce distribution by category, Email Campaigns hold the highest share at 21.49%, reflecting their significant impact in the industry. The data reveals a diverse distribution, with Warehousing, Electronics Retailers, and C2C Marketplaces also making notable contributions around the 9% mark. The importance of digital payment solutions is marked by the presence of Buy Now Pay Later and E-Wallets, which highlight evolving consumer payment preferences. Overall, this distribution suggests a robust integration of marketing, logistics, and retail solutions in driving e-commerce trends.
