In 2023, the tourism distribution across categories shows a balanced spread with some distinct preferences. Fine Dining leads with 14.55%, closely followed by Rail & Road at 14.19%. Local Cuisine and Adventure Tours also see significant interest, each above 8%. Conversely, Budget Hotels, Mountain Destinations, and Luxury Hotels represent the lowest segments, each around 5%. While the overall trend appears stable across categories, Fine Dining and Rail & Road show the most substantial engagement, indicative of a strong preference for experiences and accessibility in travel choices.
