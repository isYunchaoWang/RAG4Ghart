In 2024, education distribution data highlights a strong emphasis on informal learning, commanding 34.93% of the total. Higher education, with a combined share of 42% across various programs, shows notable diversification, with significant representation in postgraduate (18.81%) and undergraduate (23.19%) categories. Online education stands second with 23.04%, reflecting a growing trend towards digital learning platforms. The trend suggests a balanced focus across diverse education models, indicating a flexible approach catering to varied learning needs. Formal education categories like primary and secondary hold a combined 23.08%, ensuring foundational education remains a priority.
