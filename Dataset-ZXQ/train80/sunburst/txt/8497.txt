The analysis of social media distribution by category in 2022 shows that platforms, specifically within video and content creation, dominate the landscape with a combined share of 72.65%. The highest individual category is 'Platforms' at 41.53%, followed by 'Video Platforms' and 'Content Creation' each holding 31.12%. User engagement features such as 'Likes' and 'Comments' have relatively smaller shares, underscoring a potential area for growth in interactivity. Messaging apps hold significant potential, as evidenced by their notable shares in 'WhatsApp' and 'Messaging Apps' at 24.6%. The overall distribution suggests a strong emphasis on content consumption and creation, with a balanced engagement segment that includes growth opportunities in reactions and sharing.
