In 2023, the housing market is predominantly distributed across three main categories: Commercial Real Estate (38.16%), Rental Market (31.46%), and Residential Property (30.38%). Within these, Retail Spaces account for a significant portion of Commercial Real Estate at 32.63%. The Rental Market is majorly comprised of Long-Term Rentals (31.46%). Residential Property includes Single Family Homes, Apartments, and Multi-Family Homes, with the latter two each capturing 18.12%.

Overall, Commercial Real Estate leads the market share, revealing its dominant role in the housing sector. There isn't a significant fluctuation or abrupt change noted in the categories, suggesting a stable distribution pattern throughout the year. The metrics and distribution indicate a balanced demand across various housing categories, with commercial and rental markets slightly leading due to retail and long-term rental viability.
