In 2021, the transportation distribution was heavily dominated by air transport (39.13%) and urban mobility (35.69%), with public transit (30.62%) and rail freight (25.18%) also playing significant roles. The data indicates a preference for air and urban transit logistics, with a notable infrastructure investment in these sectors. Smaller categories like bike sharing and micromobility, each slightly above 5%, show a growing but still limited presence. This distribution suggests a balanced yet strategic focus on different transportation modes, with air and public transit leading as the most utilized categories.
