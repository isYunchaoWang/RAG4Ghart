In the science distribution by category for 2021, the Physical Sciences hold the largest share at 48.72%, indicating a dominant focus in this area. Applied Sciences follow with 28.88%, and within Engineering, Electrical holds 15.36%, showcasing a notable emphasis. Life Sciences comprise 22.4%, with Biology and Ecology each at 9.45%. Neuroscience, with a 12.95% presence, highlights an emerging interest, particularly in subfields like Brain Function (6.6%) and Neural Networks (6.35%). The distribution reflects a diverse yet concentrated attention on both foundational and innovative scientific domains.
