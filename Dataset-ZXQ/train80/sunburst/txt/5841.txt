In the 2021 employee distribution by category, "Workplace Wellbeing" emerged as the predominant focus at 43.21%, followed closely by "Employee Development" at 34.88%. These categories are significantly prioritized compared to others, such as "Recruitment" (12.25%) and "Ergonomics" (5.2%). Overall, there is a substantial emphasis on well-being and development, indicating a strategic focus on enhancing employee satisfaction and growth. The trend highlights the growing importance of work-life balance and flexible work arrangements, as reflected by categories like "Work-Life Balance" (32.22%) and "Flexible Scheduling" (32.22%).
