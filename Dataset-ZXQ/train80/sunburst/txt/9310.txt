In 2022, Hospitality Services emerged as the dominant category in tourism distribution, capturing 45.27% of the share. Lodging Options followed, holding a significant portion at 33.41%. Other categories such as Transportation (17.34%), Urban Tourism (22.85%), and Dining Experiences (11.86%) also contributed notably, although to a lesser extent. Air Travel reported the lowest share at 7.57%. Overall, the data indicates a clear emphasis on foundational elements like hospitality and accommodation within the tourism sector. The distribution implies stability and concentration in key areas rather than diverse expansion across smaller categories.
