In 2021, transportation distribution was dominated by air transport, accounting for 41.35% of the total, indicating its significant role. Urban mobility, comprising micromobility (24.57%) and public transit (11.28%), collectively represented a substantial portion. Road and rail freight made up smaller segments, at 5.49% and 17.31% respectively. Micromobility emerged as a notable category within urban transport. Overall, the data highlights air transport's dominance, with varied urban mobility options contributing to a diverse transportation landscape.
