The 2024 e-commerce distribution by category illustrates a diversified allocation across several sectors. Marketplace Platforms, as the category leader at 42.58%, significantly outpace others, indicating their dominant role. Fashion Retailers and B2C Platforms follow, each occupying 34.73%, revealing strong consumer interest in online fashion. Digital Payment Systems and Ethical Apparel also hold substantial shares of 21.89% and 18.94%, respectively, reflecting growth in secure transactions and sustainable fashion. Although smaller, segments like Luxury Brands (10.28%) and E-Wallets (7.08%) still play critical roles. The category with the least allocation is Warehousing at 5.02%, highlighting potential areas for infrastructure growth. Overall, the data suggests a stable diversification trend across e-commerce categories, with dominant and emerging sectors showing clear opportunities for investment and development.
