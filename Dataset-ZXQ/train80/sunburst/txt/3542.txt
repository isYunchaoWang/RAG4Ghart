The food industry distribution for 2023 is dominated by Food Processing, which comprises 49.18% of the total. This indicates a significant influence in the sector. The next largest segments are Food Production at 30.19% and Distribution and Wholesale, each contributing 20.63%. The data shows a concentration in processing and production activities, with smaller contributions from Dairy Processing (25.37%) and Meat Processing (23.81%). Notably, Aquaculture contributes 19.21%, reflecting a substantial role in primary production. These figures suggest that, while processing holds a strong position, there is a balanced distribution across various segments, with each playing a crucial role in the industry.
