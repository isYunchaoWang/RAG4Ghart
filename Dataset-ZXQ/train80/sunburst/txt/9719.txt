In 2021, the distribution of transportation by category showed significant discrepancies. Rail Freight and Urban Mobility dominated, each accounting for 38.89% and 36.38% respectively. The smallest subcategories included Underground Metro and Monorail Systems, both near the 5% range. Micromobility, encompassing E-Scooters and Bike Sharing, collectively contributed 11.13%, indicating emerging trends in urban transport. The data suggests a stable and diversified distribution with distinct peaks in logistics and urban commuting sectors, reflecting significant reliance on these categories for transportation needs.
