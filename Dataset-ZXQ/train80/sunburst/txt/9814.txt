In 2021, the transportation sector was predominantly dominated by Freight and Logistics, constituting 46.44% of the distribution, marking it as the category with the highest share. The Urban Mobility sector followed with 31.66%, driven by significant contributions from micromobility (22.35%), including e-scooters (16.29%). Air Transport comprised 21.89%, evenly split between commercial airlines and air cargo. Notable insights include consistent distributions across categories, without extreme fluctuations, indicating a balanced transportation landscape. The presence of emerging platforms, such as rental platforms and micromobility, illustrates a diversification trend within urban mobility, suggesting adaptive strategies to meet evolving transportation needs.
