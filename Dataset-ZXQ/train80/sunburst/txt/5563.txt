In the analysis of the employee distribution by category for 2021, significant investment was seen in Employee Development, accounting for the highest percentage at 40.58%. This is followed by contributions to the Employee Lifecycle and Workplace Wellbeing/Work-Life Balance, both reflecting notable allocations. The lowest percentage was observed in Goal Setting at 5.03%, indicating relatively less focus in this area. The distribution highlights a strategic emphasis on development and support initiatives, with a diverse but balanced allocation across other categories, ensuring a holistic approach to employee engagement and growth.
