The analysis of virtual housing prices by property type reveals distinct differences between farmhouses and mobile homes. Farmhouses tend to have higher price variability, with values ranging from approximately 400 to 1600 thousand USD, and a median value around 700 thousand USD. The pricing distribution for farmhouses is broader, suggesting a wider range of valuations. In contrast, mobile homes have a narrower price range, with values between approximately 300 and 900 thousand USD and a median around 600 thousand USD. The price distribution for mobile homes is more concentrated, indicating relatively stable pricing compared to farmhouses. Overall, farmhouses show a more diversified pricing trend, while mobile homes exhibit a more stable pricing pattern.
