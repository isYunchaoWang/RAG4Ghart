The discipline-wise research output distribution from 2020 to 2024 shows a clear preference for Patent outputs, which account for the largest share at 21%. This is followed by PhD Theses at 19% and Publications at 16%. Conference Papers and Innovative Solutions are equally represented, each at 11%, reflecting moderate emphasis in these areas. Research Grants and Collaborative Research are the least emphasized, at 14% and 8% respectively. Overall, there's a notable focus on tangible outcomes like Patents and PhD Theses, suggesting an emphasis on generating valuable contributions and advancing academic qualifications.
