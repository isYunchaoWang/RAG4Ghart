The 2024 global social media user share analysis reveals that TikTok leads with 43%, followed by Snapchat at 38%, and Facebook at 19%. The distribution of user share demonstrates a clear preference for TikTok, with Snapchat also holding a significant portion. Facebook, while still relevant, trails behind the other two platforms in terms of user share. There are no dramatic fluctuations or turning points, and the shares appear stable, indicating an overall sustained preference for TikTok and Snapchat among global users.
