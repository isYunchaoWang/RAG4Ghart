Over the analyzed period, housing prices for both detached houses and cottages have followed distinct patterns. Detached house prices exhibited a slight decrease initially, followed by a steady increase towards 2024, reflecting an overall upward trend with a key turning point around 2017. In contrast, cottage prices displayed more stability with a consistent increase, peaking in 2023 before stabilizing slightly lower in 2024. The data suggests a growing preference for cottages over time, evidenced by the higher growth rate and larger price fluctuation compared to detached houses. Overall, the trend highlights an increasing investment interest and demand in the cottage market segment, particularly evident in recent years.
