From 1960 to 1989, overall library visits displayed a generally increasing trend with noticeable fluctuations. The largest growth appeared between the late 1970s and early 1980s, driven primarily by increased visits to the Meridian Academy Collection and Celestial College Archives. Throughout the period, visits to different libraries experienced minor ups and downs, indicating changes in popularity. Despite these fluctuations, the overall trend was positive, suggesting increased overall engagement with these libraries over the three decades.
