In 2024, the distribution of cultural activities is dominated by artwork sales at 93.71%, highlighting its pivotal role. Within this segment, the secondary market leads with 64.18%, driven by auction houses at 55.96%, specifically focusing on modern masters (34.13%) and emerging artists (21.83%). The primary market constitutes 29.53% of the distribution, with significant contributions from galleries (19.58%), divided into traditional (10.44%) and contemporary (9.14%) art. Art funds and art fairs contribute smaller shares at 8.22% and 9.95%, respectively. Cultural activities make up the remaining 6.29%. This distribution reflects a diverse yet concentrated market, with a strong emphasis on established sales channels.
