In the 2021 education distribution by category, the Learning Systems category dominated with a 100% total, distributed primarily into Pedagogy (73.40%) and Experiential (26.60%) learning. Within Pedagogy, Competency-Based Education and Micro-Credentials both have significant shares at 42.25%, with Smart Contracts at 31.77%. In the Experiential category, Fieldwork Tech and Lab Simulations are noteworthy, with Fieldwork Tech containing sub-categories like IoT Kits and LoRa Modules. The data reveals a structured allocation with clear emphasis on digital and technology-enhanced learning methods. The lack of significant fluctuations suggests a stable approach to educational distribution, reflecting a balanced integration of traditional and innovative practices.
