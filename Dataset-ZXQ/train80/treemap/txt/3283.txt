In 2021, "Education Distribution by Category" reveals that the predominant allocation is towards Learning Systems, capturing 100% of the focus. Within this, Pedagogy is the largest subcategory, representing 79.52%, followed by Competency-Based and Micro-Credentials (both at 68.64%). Smart Contracts also hold a significant portion with 63.16%. The Experiential category, while comprising a smaller segment at 20.48%, includes notable investments in Lab Simulations (12.67%) and various technological aids like Fieldwork Tech and VR Environments. The chart indicates a substantial emphasis on traditional and competency-based learning models, with moderate integration of experiential and technological advancements. The data reflects a stable approach with slight diversification into innovative educational techniques. Overall, the focus is heavily skewed towards established methodologies with emerging trends in experiential learning beginning to take hold.
