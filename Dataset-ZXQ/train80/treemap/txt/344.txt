In 2021, agricultural production distribution saw a nearly even split between Crop Systems, with Precision Planting at 51.54% and Irrigation Technology at 48.46%. The highest sub-category within Precision Planting was Seeders and Row Units at 38.30%, whereas Pivot Systems led within Irrigation Technology at 25.30%. Significant components included Parallel Linkages at 20.74%, and Valve Types at 14.85%. Fertigation Systems and Drip Networks contributed comparably, each around 13.24% and 23.16% respectively. This distribution highlights a balanced focus on both planting precision and irrigation efficiency in agricultural practices.
