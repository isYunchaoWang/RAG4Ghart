In 2021, Science Distribution by Category highlights Physics Research as the leading focus, accounting for 87.01% of the distribution. Within this realm, Particle Accelerators and Magnet Systems are the dominant subcategories, each holding 51.63%. Superconducting, a key component, consists of 37.81%, with Quench Protection and Detection Circuits each at 29.95%. Plasma Physics and its subdivisions also feature prominently at 35.38%. Quantum Computing captures 12.99%, driven by 7.18% in Qubit Tech. Overall, the data suggests a concentrated emphasis on Physics Research, particularly in the areas of magnet systems and acceleration technologies, indicating an increasing trend towards advancements in these specialized sectors.
