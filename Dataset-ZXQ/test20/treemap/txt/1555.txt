The distribution of cultural activities in 2024 reflects a balanced engagement across various domains. Art Exhibitions dominate, accounting for 34.90% of the activities, with Online Exhibitions leading at 24.02%. The Public Art sector, particularly Urban Sculpture, also plays a significant role at 28.07% and 16.03%, respectively. Artwork Sales make up 37.03%, with a notable emphasis on the Secondary Market at 28.57%, led by Auction Houses at 18.27%. Modern Masters and Emerging Artists contribute meaningfully with 11.50% and 6.77%. Art Funds have a more measured presence at 10.30%, with close contributions from Experimental and Investment Grade funds. This distribution indicates a diverse cultural engagement, with a significant inclination towards exhibitions and the secondary art market.
