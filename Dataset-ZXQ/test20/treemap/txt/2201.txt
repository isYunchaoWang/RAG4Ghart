In the 2024 distribution of cultural activities, the most significant category is Cultural Activities at 72.81%, with Art Exhibitions forming a substantial part of it at 64.63%. Within Art Exhibitions, Gallery Shows and Online Exhibitions are sizable segments, at 36.64% and 27.99% respectively, with Virtual Reality accounting for 22.68% within Online Exhibitions. Artwork Sales represent the remaining 27.19%, dominated by the Primary Market (21.83%), featuring contributions from Art Fairs at 12.38%. The minimum values are seen in specialized areas like Regional Art Fairs (6.05%) and Secondary Art Market at 5.36%. The data suggests a stable structure with dominance in exhibitions, particularly online, highlighting a shift towards digital experiences in cultural activities.
