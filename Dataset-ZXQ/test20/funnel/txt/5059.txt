The Beverage Sales Process analysis reveals a decreasing trend through its stages. Starting with a Retail Placement of 4,562, there is a notable decline at each stage, with the figures reducing to 3,481 for In-Store Promotion, 2,207 for Customer Purchase, and finally 1,197 at Repeat Purchase. This funnel-shaped trend signifies a consistent drop-off at each step, with the most significant reduction, approximately a 46% decrease, observed from Customer Purchase to Repeat Purchase. The data indicates that while retail strategy is effective at placement and promotion, conversion to initial and repeat purchases can be significantly improved.
