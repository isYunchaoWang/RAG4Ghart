The analysis of housing price fluctuations across Coastal Properties, Luxury Residences, and Urban Redevelopment Areas reveals distinct patterns. Coastal Properties exhibit moderate fluctuation with a stable upward trend towards the end. Luxury Residences show variability, with a peak in the mid-range followed by a slight decline, indicating a fluctuating trend with a temporary increase. Urban Redevelopment Areas display significant growth initially, peaking sharply before stabilizing. Overall, the most significant growth rates are observed in Urban Redevelopment Areas, suggesting high volatility yet strong potential for investment returns in specific periods.
