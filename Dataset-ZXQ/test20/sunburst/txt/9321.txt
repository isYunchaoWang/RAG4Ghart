In the 2022 tourism distribution analysis, urban tourism emerged as the dominant category with a notable 31.88%, and city landmarks within this category contributed significantly at 13.78%. Lodging options and hospitality services also played crucial roles, comprising 20.51% and 32.5% respectively. The data reflects a strong preference for urban experiences and accommodation services. Nature tourism was the least popular at 1.2%. Overall, the distribution highlights a stable preference trend towards urban and service-oriented tourism experiences.
