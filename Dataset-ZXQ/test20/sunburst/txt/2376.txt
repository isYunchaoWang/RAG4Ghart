In the 2024 education distribution by category, data shows that informal, online, and skill platform-based learning each command a significant 50.6% share, highlighting an emerging preference towards flexible learning solutions. Skillshare emerges as a prominent player in the skill platforms segment with 30.61%. Formal education accounts for 26.39%, with higher education making up 23%. Within formal education, primary education constitutes 17.02%, while secondary and higher education makeup a smaller portion. The distribution suggests a shift towards non-traditional modes of education, with skill and online platforms playing a pivotal role in shaping educational landscapes.
