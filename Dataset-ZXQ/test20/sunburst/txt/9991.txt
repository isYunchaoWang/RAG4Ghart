In the 2021 transportation distribution analysis, Urban Mobility and Micromobility categories are tied with the highest share at 39.62% each, indicating a significant emphasis on these modes. Road Freight follows with 25.88%, showing it as a key player in the logistics sector. The total distribution emphasizes a balanced allocation across various categories with notable contributions from Air Transport (22.94%) and Freight and Logistics (37.44%). A stable trend is observed with no distinct turning points, reflecting steady distribution across categories. This balanced distribution suggests a diversified approach to transportation needs, aligning with a focus on both efficiency and flexibility in movement.
