In 2021, the transportation distribution was dominated by the Freight and Logistics category, which accounted for 45.32% of the total. Air Transport and Urban Mobility followed, contributing 31.82% and 22.85%, respectively. Among subcategories, Rail Freight held the largest share, driving the overall percentage for Freight and Logistics. Passenger Flights and Commercial Airlines were equal at 18.05%, while Air Cargo had a notable presence at 13.77%. The data suggests a diversified transportation landscape with significant emphasis on logistics and mobility solutions, especially rail and air. The range of transportation methods highlights the industry's adaptation to various market needs.
