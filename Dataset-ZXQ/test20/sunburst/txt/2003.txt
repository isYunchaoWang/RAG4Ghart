In 2024, the education distribution predominantly favors informal learning, which constitutes 36.47% of the total, while formal education accounts for 29.94%. A notable portion, 24.14%, is attributed to online education, with skill platforms such as LinkedIn Learning and Udemy being significant contributors. Community learning follows at 12.33%, evidencing a diverse array of educational methods. Public and private primary education together comprise 20.61%. The distribution highlights a growing preference for informal and online education methods, indicating a shift in learning trends toward more flexible and accessible options. Higher and undergraduate education share an equal percentage, reflecting a stable interest in traditional educational pathways.
