In 2024, energy distribution is predominantly led by the manufacturing sector at 16.04%, followed closely by mining at 15.76%. Solar energy represents a significant share at 11.55%, while thermal plants contribute 12.51%. Hydropower accounts for 10.24%, indicating diverse energy sources. The lowest contributions come from high voltage lines and wind, with 5.12% and 5.08%, respectively. This distribution reflects a varied energy mix, emphasizing both traditional and renewable sources without significant fluctuations, suggesting a stable transition toward renewable energy.
